Overview

Pay sure is a uitility payment playform that brings daily life expenses and transactions close to its users. It provides a seamless means of taking care of recurring bills and expenses.

The problem

Keeping up with expenses could be very painstaking due to the number of needs we have in our daily life, it is only important that there are easier ways to both manage and cater for these needs without been caught off guard or having to go through difficulties when settling them.

Goals

  1. Create an all in one payment platform for all bills and expenses.
  2. Design a more user friendly design that top competition.

Design process

User flow

Wireframes

Wireframes help me focus on key functions, elements, and actions before starting the visual design. I created High-Fidelity wireframes using some dummy texts and icons where needed.

Colors and Typography

Designing the visuals became very interesting after being able to validate the wireframes with potential testers of the app. I proceeded to design a simple design style guide for the application.

Visual Design

After several rounds of iterations on the wireframe, we designed the high-fidelity screens.

Conclusion

The UI design was framed based on data collected over a long period of time and multiple test sessions. The design was made to be minimal, well organized flows, and feature centered to meet the users needs. As a result, Paysure is a stylish and sophisticated web application that offers a good range of different features to create an outstanding user experience for its users with an elegant and simplistic design.
