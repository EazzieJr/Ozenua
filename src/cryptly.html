Cryptly: Creating true experience with Cryptocurrency.

  • UX Reasearch
  • UI Design
  • Visual Design
  • Prototype

Overview

Cryptly is a platform that allows users to buy, convert, invest money, conduct payments, and get the latest market statistic even for unbanked people from third world countries. Cryptly aims to make transactions very seamless, fast, reliable and hassle-free and secure for all its users by providing easy onboarding, current market statistics, price update tracking, investment education and clear text information.

Cryptly also aims to solve daily user experience issues experienced on many blockchain platforms.

The problem

During my research, I discovered that crypto wallet users have need of this few functionalities majorly, this includes: the wallet balance (portfolio), transaction history, sending and receiveing of currencies, and the wallet backup feature. After testing on about 5 different crypto wallets, I was able to validate some major pain points such as, inability to keep up with losses or gain in actual fiat value, too many features on the apps and many things too complex to understand by new cryptocurrency users.

Pain points for users on other platforms

  1. Non-educational onboarding process.
  2. Difficulty in account recovery.
  3. Difficulty in tracking portfolio.
  4. Error handling and feedbacks.
  5. Too many complex features.

Goals

  1. Non-educational onboarding process.
  2. Difficulty in account recovery.
  3. Difficulty in tracking portfolio.
  4. Error handling and feedbacks.
  5. Too many complex features.

The Solution

Cryptly was designed to provide simple user experience to it’s users by creating an experience the unschooled can have access to and use conviniently by simplifying every process from the onboarding to making transactions.

Onboarding

With a more generic onboarding the users can join the app using just their name, email and get sercurity authentication through a OTP code. Users are also prompted to choose or create a customized username which will mimick their payment addresses.

Navigation and Home page

Cryptly was designed for new Cryptocurrency users who originally would have little idea of what to look at or what actions to take. The home screen is designed to have your current balances reflect how much you have on your portfolio, how much you’ve earned or loss in percentage and as well the actual amount in your original currency. The CTAs as well are boldly placed so users can take quick action as they use the app.

Send coins

The existing homepage design on the app was full of clusters of unused items and confusing app navigation. This was highlighted to be one of the app's pain points as users were unable to locate key features needed to perform tasks. This problem was solved by removing the unused features, we paid more priority to the features that are most needed by the customers
