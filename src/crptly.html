Overview

Cryptly is a platform that allows users to buy, convert, invest money, conduct payments, and get the latest market statistic even for unbanked people from third world countries. Cryptly aims to make transactions very seamless, fast, reliable and hassle-free and secure for all its users by providing easy onboarding, current market statistics, price update tracking, investment education and clear text information.

Cryptly also aims to solve daily user experience issues experienced on many blockchain platforms.

The problem

During my research, I discovered that crypto wallet users have need of this few functionalities majorly, this includes: the wallet balance (portfolio), transaction history, sending and receiveing of currencies, and the wallet backup feature. After testing on about 5 different crypto wallets, I was able to validate some major pain points such as, inability to keep up with losses or gain in actual fiat value, too many features on the apps and many things too complex to understand by new cryptocurrency users.

Pain points for users on other platforms

  1. Non-educational onboarding process.
  2. Difficulty in account recovery.
  3. Difficulty in tracking portfolio.
  4. Error handling and feedbacks.
  5. Too many complex features.
